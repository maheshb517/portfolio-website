About Me

I am a final-year B.Tech Computer Science student at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, specializing in software development, cloud infrastructure, and AI/ML applications. With a strong foundation in Python, Java, and data-driven problem-solving, I have built projects ranging from insurance premium prediction systems to deep learning-based image enhancement and captioning, showcasing my ability to design and deploy impactful solutions.

These roles helped me gain practical expertise in automation, testing, cloud deployment, and scalable application development, while also strengthening my adaptability in both on-site and remote environments.

Beyond academics and internships, I have taken on leadership responsibilities as Media Team Lead at SRM Sketch Club, honing my team coordination and communication skills. Certified in AI/ML, Python for Data Science, and Java Development, I am eager to contribute as a Software Engineer or DevOps Engineer, combining my technical expertise and collaborative mindset to deliver innovative technology solutions.
